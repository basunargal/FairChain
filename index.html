Mission Statement

WE BELIEVE A LIVING INCOME IS A HUMAN RIGHT AND THROUGH MANY PROJECTS WE AIM TO ACHIEVE THIS. LIVING INCOME IS AT THE HEART OF OUR BUSINESS MODEL

Methodology

Through strategies, such as providing technical assistance to the farmers through training, supplying them with compost which is prepared out of the coffee waste, providing quality planting materials by setting up nurseries at multiple locations, reliable market support, shift to coffee agroforestry in some cases, and by setting up coffee roasting facilities, this project aims to increase and diversify revenues for farmers toward a liveable wage

Overview

The presented graphs illustrate the advantages of FairChain's initiatives on farmers' yield, income, CO2e sequestration, and farm biodiversity.

Scenarios

Business as usual: Depicts the projected outcomes if farmers persist in their current practices without external interventions.

Compost: Showcases the advantages of applying compost to the farm.

Technical and market support: Demonstrates the benefits of enhanced prices and the improvement of farmers' technical skills.

Combined: Displays the cumulative benefits derived from compost application, enhanced produce prices, and increased technical capabilities among farmers.
